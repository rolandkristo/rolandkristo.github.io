Roland Kristo

PhD Candidate in Political Science

University College London

I am a PhD candidate in Political Science at UCL, funded by the Clara Collet Departmental Scholarship. My research focuses on the political economy of the labour market. I hold an MSc in Data Science and Public Policy (Distinction) from UCL and a BSc in Philosophy, Politics and Economics (First Class) from King's College London. I have methodological training in quantitative and qualitative methods, causal inference, and machine learning.

Beyond academia, I am part of the senior leadership of the Centre for Democracy and Good Governance, a Romanian think tank funded by the Romanian-American Foundation.
